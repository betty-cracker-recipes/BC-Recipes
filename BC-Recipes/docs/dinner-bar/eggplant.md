# Eggplant Parm

Eggplant Parmesan

Ingredients:
	Breadcrumbs			Eggplant
	Eggs				Vegetable Oil
	Marinara Sauce		Parmesan

Instructions:
Preheat oven to 350
Slice eggplant and dip in egg & breadcrumbs
Add oil to pan
Cook covered and add to glass dish
Layer with marinara sauce 
Top with parmesan and cook covered for 30 minutes
